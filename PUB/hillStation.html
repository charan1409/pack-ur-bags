Kulu Manali,India

Popular for its scenic beauty, flowery gardens, snow-capped mountains, and orchards of red and green apples, Manali is one of the best hill stations in India in May & June. This is one of the highest hill station in India.

Gulmarg,India

Literally meaning the ‘meadow of flowers’, Gulmarg is known for its unparalleled natural beauty. A popular ski destination during winters, its Pine forests and wild flowers make for a breathtaking summer trip too. This is another best hill station in India.

Lecco,Italy

Lecco is blessed to be pleasant throughout the year and each season has something in store for you. From skiing in the winter season to soaking in the beauty of greenery during the summer season, Lecco is worth visiting in every season.

Wengen

A popular skiing destination in Europe, Wengen hosts Lauberhorn Ski races and FIS Alpine Ski World Cup every January. Apart from that, Wengen also offers countless natural hideouts for trekkers and hikers. The mesmerising landscapes and enchanting lakes make up for it to be a popular romantic destination.

Innsbruck

An Alpine city, Innsbruck is a blend of old and new. With old town and heritage still narrating the tales of past, the new part of Innsbruck offers all the luxury you could dream of. A popular ski destination, Innsbruck offers numerous other adventure options, including trekking and Ski jump.

Garmisch-Partenkirchen

If you are one of those travellers who seek natural paradise and a place which you can call your own, Garmisch-Partenkirchen is the destination made for you. With pristine lakes, high mountains and adventurous escapades, this place will make your trip a memorable affair.

Bukovel

Ask anyone and they will tell that the major reason they visited Bukovel is for its enchanting ski slopes and lavish ski resorts. The slopes here excites novice and professionals both. Apart from that, Bukovel is perfect for summer trekking. The place also offers other adventure options like mountaineering, rafting and mountain biking.

Gurez Valley

Gurez Valley is an unexplored land in the valley of Himalayas. Closed for tourists till 2007, the mystique of the valley is what attracts the tourists. The place is yet to be fully explored and this is what makes this place amazing. You can be the first in your gang to explore this hidden wonderland.
